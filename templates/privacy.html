Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect your phone number when you create an account or place an order, your delivery address, and details of your orders. For farmers, we collect farm name, location, and product listings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your phone number to verify your identity, send order updates, and process payments. Your address is shared only with the farmer and courier fulfilling your order.

3. Data Protection

SokoShamba complies with the Kenya Data Protection Act. Your personal data is stored securely and is not sold to third parties. We retain order data for at least one year for dispute resolution.

4. M‑Pesa Transactions

Payment information is processed by Safaricom's M‑Pesa service. We do not store your M‑Pesa PIN or full payment card details.

5. Communications

We send SMS notifications for order status updates, OTP verification, and important platform announcements. You may opt out of marketing messages by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@sokoshamba.co.ke.

7. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries: support@sokoshamba.co.ke
